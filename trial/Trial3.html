PROCESS

The only criteria for this project was to incorporate the idea of chance.

I began by writing on post-its things that came to mind when thinking about the word.

Rebirth is a web experiment about having another shot at life. You start as a child and risk the chance of dying at each press of the button. There are 50 emotions you can land on and 750 different lines of story. Experience how your new life unfolds by either continuously clicking live or pressing auto.
